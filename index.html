VETTEC

A new bill being introduced to help veterans attend code schools.

What's in this bill?

This bill is aimed at getting veterans into non traditional education programs like code schools.

  • 25 percent of the cost of providing the program of education upon the enrollment of an eligible veteran in the program
  • 25 percent of such cost upon the com- pletion of the program by the veteran
  • 50 percent of such cost upon the employment of the veteran following completion of the program.
  • A monthly housing stipend

Am I eligible?

An eligible veteran is a veteran who is entitled to educational assistance under the laws administered by the Secretary.

Where is this bill?

This bill was introduced to the house on 5 April 2017.
